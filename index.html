Dr. Angelina Messina

Dr. Messina was born on April 23rd, 1910 here in New York City to Sicilian immigrants. Ever since she was young, science had sparked her interest. She grew up to become one of the few female micro-paleontologists in the world. Messina is best known for her famous work on foraminifera, (also called forams), which are single-celled and shelled protists, and ostracoda, a crustacean group.
